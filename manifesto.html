The Manifesto

01. The Hybrid Promise

REVERB is not a typical tech startup; we are building a "Hybrid Model" inspired by the Mozilla ethos.

  • The Engine Operations are managed by a Public Benefit Corporation (PBC), legally bound to prioritize social good over shareholder profit.
  • The Soul Our brand, data ethics, and surplus revenue are governed by a nonprofit mandate.
  • The Commitment We do not take "dirty" VC funding, seeking independence through a transparent cover charge.

02. Data Sovereignty

We believe your data is an extension of your personhood, not a product.

  • No Extraction We will never sell, lease, or trade your information to third-party brokers.
  • Radical Minimization We only ask for what we need—your current resonance, not a "cover letter" life.
  • Ephemeral by Design Presence in the Room expires to ensure active, immediate connection.

03. The Ethos

  • Come As You Are No presentation rules; we celebrate the unvarnished self.
  • Fluidity Welcome Whether queer-curious, T4T, or seeking kink, this is a space for honest desire.
  • Safety First Zero tolerance for hate speech or corporate tourism.

04. Economics

  • Support for Trans Youth Surplus revenue is dedicated to gender-affirming care and mutual aid.
  • User Ownership We are exploring co-op structures to ensure the community has a voice.

05. Legal Shield

Until formal incorporation is finalized, you are participating in an early-stage community project. By joining the list, you hold the founders harmless from claims arising from this pre-launch phase.

By joining the guest list, you are whispering into our ear that you’re in. You agree to these terms and our radical vision for what the internet should have been.
