Rules of the Room

01. Resonance, not resumés.

Spend less time curating a perfect profile, and more time meeting with authenticity. Current photos and intentions, guaranteed.

02. Vibe-based proximity.

Stop signaling that you have it all figured out. This is about what you want, right now. We show you similar energies, with zero gatekeeping and no hidden profiles.

03. One Room. One Cover.

Pay once, stay for the night. No recurring fees, no hidden paywalls, and no tourists. Everyone here is invested in being present. Find your connection and get offline.

04. Ephemeral by design

Your presence in the room expires after 24 hours. No digital footprints, no stale profiles, and no "ghosts." If you’re here, you’re present. If you’re gone, you’re gone.
